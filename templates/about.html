What is Travelsoo ?

Travelsoo is an Indian online travel company . The company directly communicates with the customers through its verified online platform and deals with all customers’ concerns related to travelling. In other words, Travelso is the perfect guide for you to plan your vacation. Travelsoo provides places to stay, villas and homestays, inter-city cab service, holiday packages, and hotel booking services on its portal.

One of the reasons travelers choose Travelsoo is its comprehensive range of travel services. Expect to be able to plan an entire trip on here, from booking flights, hotels, and vacation packages.

Travelso describes itself as a “your-personal-travel-partner” both for locals and foreigners touring in India. It's easy to use and offers competitive prices, making it an attractive option for budget-conscious travelers.
At present, most of the travel products and services available on the site are for travelers in India. You can use the official website of Travelsoo to place and manage your reservations.
